About UUID v4

  • UUID v4 uses random/pseudo-random numbers, giving 2122 possible values
  • Collision probability is astronomically low (~1 in 5.3×1036)
  • Widely used for database primary keys, session tokens, and distributed IDs
  • RFC 4122 standard: xxxxxxxx-xxxx-4xxx-yxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx
